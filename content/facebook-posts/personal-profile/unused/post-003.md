The biggest lie in trading?

"You need a complex strategy to win."

I bought into that for years. Indicators stacked on indicators. Multiple timeframes. Custom algorithms.

Result? Paralysis.

Then I stripped it all back. One setup. Two rules. Execute or don't.

My win rate went UP.

Complexity feels smart. Simplicity makes money.

Which one are you chasing?
