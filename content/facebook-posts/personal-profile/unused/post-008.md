Three years ago I was working a job I hated, dreaming about freedom.

Today? I trade from anywhere. Coffee shops. Beaches. My couch at 2 PM on a Tuesday.

But here's what nobody shows you:

The journey between those two points is messy.

Late nights studying. Early mornings backtesting. Doubt. Frustration. Moments where you want to quit.

The lifestyle is real. But it's earned, not given.

What are you willing to sacrifice for yours?
