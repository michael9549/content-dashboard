Trading revealed something about me I didn't want to see.

I was impatient. Impulsive. Always looking for the next thing.

The market doesn't care about your personality flaws. It exploits them.

Every weakness I had in life showed up in my trading.

But here's the beautiful part:

Fix it in the markets, and you fix it everywhere else.

Trading isn't just about money. It's about becoming someone who can handle pressure.

Who are you becoming?
