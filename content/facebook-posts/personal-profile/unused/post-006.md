"What's your win rate?"

People ask me this like it's the only number that matters.

But here's what they miss:

A 40% win rate with a 2:1 reward-to-risk beats a 60% win rate with 0.5:1.

Math doesn't lie. Egos do.

Stop chasing high win rates. Start chasing positive expectancy.

One good trade can pay for three small losses.

Are you trading for your ego or your edge?
