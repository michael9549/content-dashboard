I used to check my P&L every 5 minutes during trades.

Big mistake.

Every tick against me felt like a personal attack. I'd exit early, revenge trade, or move stops.

Sound familiar?

Now? I set my stop. I set my target. And I walk away.

The market will do what it does. My job isn't to control it—it's to follow my plan.

Detach from the outcome. Attach to the process.
