Your trading plan is worthless if you don't follow it.

I know. Harsh. But true.

I've written elaborate plans. Beautiful documents with rules for every scenario.

Then the market opened and I ignored all of them.

The plan isn't the problem. The discipline is.

Write it simple enough that a 12-year-old could follow it.

Then be the person who actually does.

Are you planning or performing?
