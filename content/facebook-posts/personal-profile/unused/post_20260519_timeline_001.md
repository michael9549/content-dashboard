I was watching a video recently from a successful entrepreneur who shared something that really stuck with me.

He said the difference between people who achieve their goals and those who don't isn't talent, luck, or even intelligence. It's consistency.

The person who shows up every day, even when they don't feel like it, eventually surpasses the person who only works when motivation strikes.

Think about it. How many times have you started something with excitement, only to quit when the initial enthusiasm faded? We've all been there.

But the people who build real businesses, create real wealth, and achieve real freedom are the ones who keep going when it gets boring, hard, or frustrating.

Consistency beats intensity. Every single time.
