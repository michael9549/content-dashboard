I used to think successful traders had some secret I didn't know.

Like there was a hidden book, or a special indicator, or some piece of knowledge that once you had it, everything clicked. I spent thousands on courses looking for that secret. Convinced that the next one would be the one that finally made it all make sense.

Then one day, I was talking to a trader who had been consistently profitable for over a decade. I asked him straight up: 'What's the secret? What am I missing?' He laughed. Not in a mean way. But like he remembered asking the same question once. 'There is no secret,' he said. 'There's just the work. Most people don't want to hear that.'

I didn't believe him at first. But over time, I started to see he was right. The traders who made it weren't the ones with the most expensive courses or the fanciest indicators. They were the ones who put in the reps. Who reviewed their trades religiously. Who stayed humble and kept learning. Who showed up even when they didn't feel like it.

The 'secret' is that there is no secret. Just consistent effort over time. Are you looking for a shortcut? Or are you willing to put in the work?
