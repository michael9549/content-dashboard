The most expensive mistake I ever made had nothing to do with a bad trade.

It was the time I spent six months learning a strategy that didn't fit me. I saw someone online crushing it with scalping. Making thousands every day. And I thought, 'That's it. That's what I need to do.' So I dove in. Learned the strategy inside and out. Practiced for hours every day.

But here's what I didn't realize: that trader had a completely different personality than me. He loved the fast pace. The quick decisions. The adrenaline. I hated it. Every session felt like a battle. My stress was through the roof. And even when I won, I felt drained.

When I finally admitted that scalping wasn't for me, it felt like failure. All that time wasted. But looking back, it was the most valuable lesson I ever learned. Trading isn't about finding the 'best' strategy. It's about finding YOUR strategy. The one that fits your personality, your lifestyle, your strengths.

I switched to swing trading and never looked back. My results improved immediately. Not because the strategy was better, but because I was better with it. Are you trading a strategy that actually fits who you are? Or are you trying to be someone else?
