Nobody talks about this:

The hardest part of trading isn't finding setups.

It's sitting on your hands when there AREN'T any.

FOMO is real.

You see a move happening and you want in.

Even when it's not your setup.

Even when the risk doesn't make sense.

That's when accounts get destroyed.

The pros wait.

They know there's always another trade.

Can you relate to FOMO? How do you handle it?
