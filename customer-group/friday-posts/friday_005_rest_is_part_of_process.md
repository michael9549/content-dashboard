🛑 **Rest is Not Optional**

You know what separates good traders from great ones?

It's not screen time. It's not how many setups they take. It's not even how much they study.

It's knowing when to step AWAY.

Trading is a mental marathon, not a sprint. And if you're grinding 7 days a week, staring at charts every night, replaying trades in your head all weekend, you're not "dedicated."

You're burning out.

Your brain needs rest to process. Your emotions need space to reset. And your decision making needs recovery time, just like your body after a workout.

This weekend, I want you to actually take a break.

Not a "I'll just check the charts for 5 minutes" break. A real one.

Close your trading apps. Step away from the screens. Do something that has nothing to do with the market.

Because when Monday comes, you don't want to show up tired, emotional, and foggy. You want to show up sharp.

And that only happens if you REST.

The market will be there Monday. Your trades will be there Monday. But if you don't take care of yourself, YOU won't be there Monday. At least not the version of you that trades well.

Rest this weekend. It's part of the process, not a break from it.

💬 Comment "REST" if you're committing to an actual weekend off from the charts.
