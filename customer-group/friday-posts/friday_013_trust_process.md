🎯 **Trust the Process, Even When It's Slow**

Here's the hard truth:

You're not going to "figure out" trading in one week.

You're not going to master it in one month.

And you're definitely not going to have consistent results after just a handful of trades.

But that doesn't mean you're failing.

It means you're LEARNING. And learning takes time.

The problem is, most traders expect instant results. They want to be profitable NOW. And when it doesn't happen fast enough, they quit.

Or worse, they start changing everything. New strategy. New indicators. New setups.

And that's how you stay stuck forever.

This weekend, I want you to commit to ONE thing:

Trust your process long enough for it to actually work.

Pick your setups. Follow your rules. Journal your trades. Review your mistakes.

And do that for the next 100 trades. Not 10. Not 20. 100.

Because consistency doesn't come from jumping around. It comes from repetition.

So stop chasing magic bullets. Start trusting the process.

💬 Comment "PROCESS" if you're committing to your plan for the next 100 trades, no matter what.
