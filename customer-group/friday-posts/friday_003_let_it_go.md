🧘 **Leave It Behind**

Whatever happened this week: let it go.

The winning streak that has you feeling invincible? Let it go.  
The losing streak that has you questioning everything? Let it go.  
That one trade you're still replaying in your head? Let. It. Go.

Monday is a clean slate. You don't get bonus points for last week's wins. And you don't start in a hole because of last week's losses.

Every week is 0:0.

The traders who struggle the most are the ones who carry emotional baggage from one week into the next.

They're trading with revenge. Or overconfidence. Or fear. And none of those emotions make you better. They just cloud your judgment.

This weekend, do yourself a favor:

Close your charts. Close your brokerage app. Stop replaying trades in your head.

Go do something that has nothing to do with trading. Spend time with people you care about. Move your body. Get outside.

Because when Monday comes, you want to show up fresh. Not burned out. Not emotional. Not stuck in last week.

The market doesn't care about your last trade. And neither should you.

Reset. Recharge. Come back ready.

💬 Drop a ✅ if you're committing to a mental reset this weekend.
