🔥 **Momentum is Real**

If you had a good week, don't let it go to waste.

Most traders have a great week, celebrate for five minutes, then completely forget what they did right.

And when Monday comes, they're back to guessing.

This weekend, while it's fresh, write down EXACTLY what you did differently this week.

What setups did you take?  
What did you avoid?  
How did you manage your trades?  
What was your mindset like?

Because the goal isn't just to have ONE good week. It's to REPEAT it.

And you can't repeat what you don't remember.

Trading isn't about getting lucky once. It's about finding what works and doing it over and over until it becomes automatic.

So if this week felt good, don't just ride the high. Lock in the lesson.

Document it. Study it. Then do it again next week.

That's how you turn a good week into a good month. And a good month into a good year.

💬 Drop a ✍️ if you're journaling what went RIGHT this week so you can do it again.
