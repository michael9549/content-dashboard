🎯 **Don't Miss the Small Wins**

Before you close out the week, take 60 seconds and write down ONE thing you did well.

Not your biggest trade. Not your P&L. Just one small thing you're proud of.

Maybe you:

: Waited for confirmation instead of chasing  
: Cut a losing trade before it got ugly  
: Skipped a setup that didn't fit your plan  
: Journaled every trade this week  
: Showed up even when you didn't feel like it

It doesn't have to be flashy. It just has to be real.

Because most traders only measure success by their account balance. And when the balance doesn't move fast enough, they think they're failing.

But trading is built on small, boring wins. The kind nobody sees. The kind that don't feel exciting in the moment.

Those small wins compound. They turn into consistency. And consistency turns into results.

So this weekend, give yourself credit for the little things. Because those little things? They're the BIG things in disguise.
