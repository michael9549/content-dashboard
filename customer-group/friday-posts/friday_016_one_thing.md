🎯 **This Weekend: Pick ONE Thing**

Don't try to fix everything at once.

That's how you stay overwhelmed and make no progress.

Instead, pick ONE thing you're going to improve next week.

Just one.

Maybe it's:

: Waiting for full confirmation before entering  
: Cutting losses faster when a trade goes against you  
: Journaling every trade, no exceptions  
: Avoiding revenge trades after a loss  
: Sticking to your position size rules  

Whatever it is, write it down. Make it specific. Then commit to it for the next five trading days.

Not forever. Just five days.

Because here's the thing: when you focus on improving ONE thing at a time, you actually make progress.

When you try to fix everything all at once, you just spin your wheels.

So this weekend, don't overwhelm yourself with a laundry list of changes.

Pick one. Master it. Then move to the next.

That's how you build consistency.
