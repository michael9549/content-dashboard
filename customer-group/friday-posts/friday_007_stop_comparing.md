⚠️ **Stop Comparing Your Week to Everyone Else's**

You see someone post a big win in the group and think, "Why can't I do that?"

You see someone crushing it and wonder, "What am I missing?"

Here's what you're missing: context.

You don't know how long they've been trading.  
You don't know how many losses they took before that win.  
You don't know how much they risked, or how many hours they put in, or how many mistakes they made along the way.

All you see is the highlight reel. And you're comparing it to your behind the scenes.

That's not fair. And it's not helpful.

Your only job is to be better than YOU were last week. That's it.

Did you follow your rules more consistently?  
Did you cut a bad trade faster?  
Did you wait for confirmation instead of chasing?

If the answer is yes to ANY of those, you're improving. And improvement is the only thing that matters.

This weekend, stop measuring yourself against everyone else. Measure yourself against the trader you were 30 days ago.

That's the only comparison that counts.

💬 Comment "PROGRESS" if you're focused on YOUR growth, not someone else's results.
