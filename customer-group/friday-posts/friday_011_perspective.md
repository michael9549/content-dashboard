🌎 **Zoom Out for a Second**

You know what's wild?

A year from now, you won't even remember this week.

You won't remember that losing trade that's eating at you right now. You won't remember that setup you missed. You won't remember the frustration or the doubt.

But you WILL remember whether you quit or kept going.

Trading is a long game. And most people lose because they treat every single week like it's life or death.

It's not.

This week is just one data point in a much bigger picture. And the traders who win are the ones who can take a loss, learn from it, and move forward without spiraling.

So this weekend, I want you to practice perspective.

Ask yourself:

: Will this week matter in six months?  
: Am I making progress, even if it's slow?  
: Am I still here, still learning, still showing up?

If the answer to those last two is YES, then you're winning. Even if it doesn't feel like it right now.

Keep going. The long game always rewards the people who stay in it.
