📊 **Before You Log Off This Week**

Quick question: Did you win or lose this week?

Most traders will check their P&L and call it a day.

But here's a better question:

Did you EXECUTE or did you IMPROVISE?

Because you can have a green week while breaking every rule in your playbook. And you can have a red week while trading perfectly.

The P&L is just the scoreboard. The real game is whether you stuck to your process.

This weekend, instead of obsessing over dollars, ask yourself:

✅ Did I follow my entry rules?  
✅ Did I respect my stops?  
✅ Did I size my positions correctly?  
✅ Did I take trades that fit my plan, or did I force trades because I was bored?

If you can answer YES to most of those, you had a winning week. Even if the account is red.

If you broke your rules and still made money? You got lucky. And luck runs out.

This weekend, review your execution, not just your results.

That's how you build a foundation that lasts.

💬 Comment "EXECUTED" if you stuck to your plan this week, win or lose.
