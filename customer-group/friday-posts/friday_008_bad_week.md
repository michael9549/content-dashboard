📉 **Had a Bad Week? Good.**

Not because losing feels good. But because bad weeks are where you learn the most.

When you're winning, it's easy to think you've got it all figured out. You get confident. Maybe even a little careless.

But when you lose? That's when you PAY ATTENTION.

You review your trades.  
You question your process.  
You figure out what went wrong.

And THAT'S where the growth happens.

So if this week kicked your butt, don't waste it. Use it.

This weekend, pull up your journal (you ARE journaling, right?) and ask yourself:

: What did I do that I shouldn't have?  
: What did I skip that I should have done?  
: Was I following my plan, or winging it?  
: Did I let one bad trade turn into three?

Answer those honestly, and you'll walk into Monday with clarity instead of frustration.

Bad weeks don't break you. They BUILD you. But only if you're willing to learn from them.
