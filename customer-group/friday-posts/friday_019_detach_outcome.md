🧘 **Detach from the Outcome**

Here's a tough truth:

You can do everything right and still lose.

You can follow your rules perfectly. Wait for the best setup. Size your position correctly. And still get stopped out.

And that's okay.

Because trading isn't about being right every time. It's about being right ENOUGH over time.

But most traders can't handle that. They take one loss personally. They spiral. They start doubting their plan.

And that's when they blow up.

This weekend, practice detaching from outcomes.

Stop measuring success by whether one trade won or lost. Start measuring it by whether you EXECUTED your plan.

Did you follow your rules? Then it was a good trade. Even if you lost money.

Did you break your rules? Then it was a bad trade. Even if you made money.

Once you can separate execution from results, everything gets easier.

You stop taking losses personally. You stop letting wins make you overconfident. You just trade your plan and trust the process.

That's when you become consistent.
