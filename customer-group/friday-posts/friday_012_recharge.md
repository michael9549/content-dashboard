🔋 **You Can't Trade on Empty**

If your phone battery hit 10%, would you keep using it until it died?

Or would you plug it in?

Your brain works the same way.

And if you've been grinding all week, staring at charts, replaying trades, stressing over every decision, your battery is LOW.

You might not feel it yet. But it's there. And if you don't recharge, Monday is going to be rough.

This weekend, do something that fills your tank:

: Spend time with people you care about  
: Move your body (seriously, go for a walk)  
: Do something creative  
: Get outside  
: Sleep in  
: Laugh

Whatever it is, make sure it has nothing to do with trading.

Because the best traders aren't the ones who grind 24/7. They're the ones who know when to step away so they can come back SHARP.

You can't make good decisions when you're running on fumes. So recharge this weekend. It's not optional.
