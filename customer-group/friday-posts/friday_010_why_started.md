💭 **Weekend Reminder: Why Did You Start?**

Seriously. Think about it.

Why did you decide to learn how to trade?

Was it freedom? More time with family? Financial independence? Proving something to yourself?

Whatever it was, I bet it wasn't "so I could stress about every tick and feel anxious all weekend."

But if you're honest, that's what trading has become for a lot of you.

And that's a sign you've lost sight of the bigger picture.

This weekend, I want you to reconnect with your WHY.

Not your P&L. Not your win rate. Not your account balance.

Your WHY.

Because when trading feels hard (and it will), your WHY is what keeps you going.

It's what reminds you that the struggle is temporary, but the skill you're building is permanent.

So take a minute this weekend. Write it down. Read it. Remember it.

Then come back Monday with that purpose front and center.

You didn't start this to be perfect. You started this to be FREE.

Don't forget that.
