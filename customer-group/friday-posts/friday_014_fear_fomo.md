😰 **Fear and FOMO: Pick Your Poison**

Most of your trading mistakes come from one of two emotions:

**FEAR** (you hesitate and miss good setups)  
or  
**FOMO** (you chase and take bad setups)

And here's the kicker: both of them feel REAL in the moment.

Fear whispers, "What if this one goes against you?"  
FOMO screams, "You're gonna miss it!"

And both of them make you trade emotionally instead of logically.

This weekend, think about which one is YOUR problem.

Are you the trader who freezes up and watches great setups pass by?  
Or are you the trader who jumps in too early because you can't stand missing out?

Once you know which one you struggle with, you can start fixing it.

For FEAR: remind yourself that no single trade matters. Your edge plays out over 100 trades, not one.

For FOMO: remind yourself that there will ALWAYS be another setup. Missing one trade is not the end of the world.

You can't trade well if you're being controlled by your emotions.

Figure out which emotion is running the show. Then take back control.
