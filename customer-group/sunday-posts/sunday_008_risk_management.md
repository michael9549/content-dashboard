🛡️ **This Week: Protect Your Capital**

You can have the best strategy in the world. But if you don't manage your risk, you're going to blow up.

It's that simple.

This week, your focus is RISK MANAGEMENT.

Every trade you take, ask yourself:

: How much am I risking?  
: Is this within my limits?  
: Can I afford to lose this and still trade tomorrow?

If the answer to any of those is NO, don't take the trade.

Because here's the thing: protecting your capital is MORE important than making money.

You can always make money back. But if you blow your account, you're done.

So this week, trade like someone who respects their capital.

No over sizing. No "just this once" trades. No risking more because you're down and want to make it back.

Stick to your risk rules. Every single trade. No exceptions.

That's how you survive long enough to become profitable.

💬 Comment "RISK FIRST" if you're committing to proper risk management this week.
