⏳ **This Week: Practice Patience**

You know what kills more accounts than bad setups?

Impatience.

Entering before confirmation. Chasing after the move already happened. Taking trades just because you're bored.

All of it comes down to one thing: you can't wait.

And that's going to cost you money. Every single time.

This week, your focus is PATIENCE.

Wait for your setup to fully form.  
Wait for confirmation before entering.  
Wait for the right moment instead of forcing it.

Yes, you might miss a few trades. Yes, it might feel like you're "doing nothing."

But here's the truth: the best traders are the ones who can SIT STILL.

They don't need to be in a trade every second. They're comfortable waiting for the RIGHT trade.

And that's what separates them from everyone else.

So this week, practice being patient. Even when it's boring. Even when it feels like you're missing out.

Because the trade you wait for will always be better than the one you chase.
