🚫 **This Week: No Revenge Trading**

You take a loss. It stings. And immediately, your brain says:

"I need to make that back. RIGHT NOW."

So you jump into the next trade. Without waiting. Without checking your rules. Without thinking.

And you lose again.

That's revenge trading. And it's one of the fastest ways to blow up your account.

This week, your commitment is simple:

NO REVENGE TRADING.

When you take a loss, step away. Take a breath. Reset.

Don't touch your mouse for at least 10 minutes. Go get water. Go for a walk. Do ANYTHING except immediately jump back in.

Because trades taken out of emotion NEVER end well.

You're not thinking clearly. You're not following your plan. You're just trying to feel better.

And the market doesn't care how you feel.

This week, when you lose, give yourself time to reset BEFORE the next trade.

That one habit will save you more money than any strategy ever could.

💬 Comment "NO REVENGE" if you're committing to taking a break after every loss this week.
