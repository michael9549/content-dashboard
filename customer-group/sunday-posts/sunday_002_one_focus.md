🎯 **This Week: ONE Focus**

Don't try to do everything perfectly this week.

You're not going to nail every setup. You're not going to have flawless execution. You're not going to become a consistently profitable trader overnight.

But you CAN improve ONE thing.

So before Monday opens, pick your focus for the week:

Maybe it's: "This week, I'm waiting for full confirmation before I enter."

Or: "This week, I'm cutting my losses faster."

Or: "This week, I'm journaling every single trade."

Whatever it is, make it specific. Make it measurable. And commit to it for five trading days.

Not forever. Just this week.

Because when you focus on improving one thing at a time, you actually make progress.

When you try to fix everything at once, you just overwhelm yourself and nothing sticks.

So this week, keep it simple. Pick one area to tighten up. Then execute on it every single day.

That's how you build consistency. One focused week at a time.
