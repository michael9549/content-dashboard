⚙️ **This Week: Process Over Profit**

Stop checking your P&L every five minutes.

Seriously. It's not helping you trade better. It's just making you emotional.

This week, your focus is PROCESS, not profit.

Did you follow your rules? That's a win.  
Did you wait for confirmation? That's a win.  
Did you cut a loser fast? That's a win.

Even if you lost money.

Because here's the thing: if you focus on executing your process correctly, the profit will come.

But if you focus on profit FIRST, you'll start forcing trades. Chasing. Over risking. Breaking your rules.

And that's how you lose.

So this week, measure success by your EXECUTION, not your account balance.

At the end of each day, ask yourself:

"Did I trade according to my plan?"

If the answer is YES, you had a good day. Even if you're red.

If the answer is NO, you had a bad day. Even if you're green.

Trust the process. The results will follow.

💬 Comment "PROCESS" if you're focused on execution this week, not just dollars.
