🎯 **This Week: Rules, Not Feelings**

Here's the problem with most traders:

They trade based on how they FEEL, not what their PLAN says.

They "feel" like a setup is going to work, so they enter early.  
They "feel" like a losing trade will turn around, so they hold too long.  
They "feel" like they need to trade, so they force setups that don't fit.

And feelings are a terrible trading strategy.

This week, your focus is RULES OVER FEELINGS.

Before every trade, ask yourself:

"Does this fit my plan, or am I just feeling impulsive?"

If it fits your plan? Execute with confidence.

If it's just a feeling? SKIP IT.

Because your rules are designed to keep you profitable over time. Your feelings are designed to keep you COMFORTABLE in the moment.

And comfortable doesn't make you money.

This week, trade like a robot. Follow your rules. Ignore your emotions.

Do that consistently, and you'll be shocked how much better your results get.

💬 Comment "RULES NOT FEELINGS" if you're committing to trading your plan this week, not your emotions.
