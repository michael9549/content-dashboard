📝 **This Week: Journal EVERY Trade**

I know. Journaling feels like homework.

But here's the reality: the traders who journal consistently are the ones who improve the fastest.

Because you can't fix what you don't track.

You can't identify patterns if you're not recording them. You can't see your mistakes if you're not reviewing them.

So this week, your commitment is simple:

Journal. Every. Single. Trade.

What setup did you take?  
Why did you enter?  
How did you manage it?  
What was the outcome?  
What did you do well?  
What would you do differently?

It doesn't have to be a novel. Just a few notes after every trade.

And at the end of the week, review it. Look for patterns. Look for mistakes you're repeating.

Because that's where the growth happens.

This week, don't skip it. Don't tell yourself you'll "do it later." Do it RIGHT AFTER THE TRADE.

That's how you build the habit. And that habit is what turns you into a consistent trader.
