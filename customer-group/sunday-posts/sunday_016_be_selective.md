🎯 **This Week: Be Picky**

Not every setup is worth taking.

In fact, MOST setups aren't worth taking.

And the faster you accept that, the better your trading will get.

This week, your goal is to be SELECTIVE.

Only trade the setups that check ALL your boxes.

: Fits your rules? ✅  
: Clean confirmation? ✅  
: Good risk reward? ✅  
: Confidence level HIGH? ✅

If it's missing even ONE of those, skip it.

Because mediocre setups lead to mediocre results. And you're not here to be mediocre.

You're here to be PROFITABLE. And profitability comes from trading ONLY the best opportunities.

This week, if you only take 2-3 trades because nothing else fits your criteria? That's a WIN.

Cash is a position. And sometimes the best trade is NO trade.

Be picky. Be patient. Wait for the setups that DESERVE your capital.

That's how you protect your account and grow it at the same time.
