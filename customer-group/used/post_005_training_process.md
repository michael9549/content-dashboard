Here is a simple way to tell if you are treating this like a hobby or a business.

A hobby trader logs in whenever they "feel like it." They click around the scanners, maybe take a trade, then disappear for a few days.

A serious trader runs a **training process.** Even if they are part time.

That process can be as simple as:

1. Watch one replay or training clip each day.
2. Take notes on one idea you can apply.
3. Open Sniper Pro or Quantum and go find one example of that idea on the chart.
4. Save a screenshot and add it to your playbook.

It does not matter if you are still on demo or trading small size. What matters is that you are **deliberately practicing** instead of randomly clicking.

If you do this for the next 30 days, you will:

- Recognize your setups faster
- Feel calmer when you see them
- Stop overcomplicating your day

The people in this group who look "naturally talented" did not start that way. They just committed to a process like this and stuck with it.

If you want help designing a simple training routine for your schedule, drop a "PLAN" in the comments and we will help you map one out.
