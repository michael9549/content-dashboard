Imagine if a basketball player only touched a ball on game day.

No drills. No shots in the gym. No film review.

You would never call that person serious.

Yet a lot of traders do the exact same thing. They only "practice" when real money is on the line.

Skill in this game is built in the **reps you do when nothing is at stake.**

Here are a few simple reps you can run this week:

- Mark 10 clean examples of your favorite setup using Sniper Pro, Quantum, and ATH
- Look for confluence between the tools to confirm your setups
- Write one or two bullet points about why you liked each trade

Do that a few times a week and you will build a playbook that:

- Makes you faster at spotting opportunities
- Keeps you out of low quality trades
- Gives you confidence when it is time to click the button

You do not have to overhaul your life. You just have to commit to small, consistent reps.

Comment "REPS" if you are down to build real skill, not just chase random wins.
