# My Simple 15-Minute Pre-Market Routine

People ask me all the time: "What's your morning routine?"

Here's the honest answer: It's not fancy. It's not complicated. But it's consistent.

**My 15-minute pre-market routine:**

**Minute 0-5: Run the Scanners**
I run Sniper Pro, Quantum, and ATH on my top 5-7 pairs. I'm not looking for trades yet. I'm just seeing what's setting up. What's forming. Where the tools are aligning. What's worth watching.

**Minute 5-10: Check Discord**
I skim the overnight chatter. Any news I missed? Any setups other people are watching? This keeps me connected to the community pulse.

**Minute 10-15: Set My Intentions**
I write down: My max trades for the day. My risk per trade. My goal (not profit goal... process goal). Then I walk away until London or NY open.

That's it. 15 minutes.

**Here's what I DON'T do:**
- I don't scan 50 pairs
- I don't watch 15-minute charts all morning
- I don't chase every alert that pops up
- I don't trade the Asian session (usually)

**Your edge comes from what you DON'T do, not what you do.**

The routine isn't sexy. But it works because I do it every single day.
