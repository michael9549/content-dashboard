If your last week of trading was messy, this is your permission slip to reset.

A bad week does not mean you are a bad trader. It means you:

- Broke your rules
- Traded when you were tired or emotional
- Ignored what Sniper Pro and Quantum were clearly telling you

Instead of beating yourself up, do this:

1. Pick one day from last week.
2. Rewatch it with the scanners open, but do not place any trades.
3. Ask, "If I only took the clean signals that matched my rules, what would my day have looked like?"

Most of the time, you will see that your worst days were not because the market was out to get you. They were because you tried to outsmart your own system.

You do not need a new strategy every time you feel pain. You need a better commitment to the one you already have.

Comment "RESET" if you are giving yourself a fresh start this week and coming back to the basics.
