Have you ever stared at a level on your chart and thought:

"Is this really important, or am I just drawing lines to feel busy?"

That is exactly why the ATH indicator exists.

It is there to help you:

- Stop guessing which highs and lows matter
- See where price has made real decisions before
- Build more confidence around your entries and exits

Here is a simple exercise for you this week:

1. Drop ATH on your favorite pair.
2. Before you take any trade, pause and ask: "What is ATH telling me about this area?"
3. If price is smashing straight into a key level, maybe you wait for confirmation.
4. If price is breaking away from a level with strength, maybe that is your green light.

The goal is not to trade every single touch. The goal is to **frame your ideas around structure that actually matters.**

If you feel like you have been forcing trades in the middle of nowhere, use ATH to slow yourself down and anchor your decisions.
