# The 10-Minute Habit That Separates Amateur Traders From Pros

At the end of every trading day, pros do something amateurs don't.

They review their trades.

Not for hours. Not with fancy software. Just 10 minutes of honest reflection.

---

**Here's what a daily review looks like:**

What trades did I take today?
Did I follow my plan on each one?
What did I do well?
What would I do differently?
What will I focus on tomorrow?

That's it. 10 minutes. In a notebook. In a spreadsheet. Doesn't matter.

---

**Why this matters:**

You can't improve what you don't measure.

Without review, you repeat the same mistakes for months without realizing it. You tell yourself stories that aren't true. You blame the market instead of looking in the mirror.

A daily review forces honesty. It creates awareness. It turns experience into wisdom.

---

**The traders who skip this step:**

They stay stuck. They make the same errors. They wonder why they're not improving.

10 minutes a day = 40 hours of intentional practice per year.

That's a full work week dedicated to getting better.

---

**You don't need more strategies. You need more self-awareness.**

Start tonight.
