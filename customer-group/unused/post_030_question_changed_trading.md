# The Question That Changed My Trading

I want to share a question that completely shifted how I approach trading...

**"Would I take this trade if nobody would ever know about it?"**

Think about that for a second.

No Discord to post the win in. No screenshot to share. No one to impress. Just you, the chart, and the trade.

Would you still take it?

**Most traders trade for validation, not profit.**

They take trades so they have something to post about. They size up so they have bigger wins to share. They trade when they should be sitting out because they don't want to feel like they're "missing out."

I know because I did this for years.

The moment I started asking myself "would I take this trade in complete isolation?" everything changed.

My trade quality went up. My size went down (because I wasn't trying to impress anyone). My consistency improved.

**The best traders I know trade like nobody's watching. Because essentially, nobody is.**

Your P&L doesn't care about your followers. Your account balance doesn't care about your reputation. The market definitely doesn't care.

So before you take your next trade, ask yourself: Would I take this if nobody would ever know?

If the answer is no... don't take it.
