# Stop Comparing Your Chapter 2 to Someone Else's Chapter 20

Social media makes it look like everyone is winning except you.

Screenshots of big wins.
Stories of "how I made $10K this month."
Traders posting their "easy" setups that somehow you never see.

And there you are, struggling to string together two green days.

Here's what you need to remember:

You're comparing your behind-the-scenes to their highlight reel.

---

**The trader posting huge wins?**

You don't see:
→ The years of losses that came before
→ The blown accounts
→ The emotional breakdowns
→ The 3am study sessions

You see the result.
Not the process.

---

**Your journey is YOUR journey.**

Your timeline. Your lessons. Your pace.

The only person you need to be better than is the trader you were last month.

Everyone else is running their own race.

---

**Drop a 🙌 if you're done comparing and ready to focus on YOUR progress.**

Your chapter 20 is coming. Keep writing.
