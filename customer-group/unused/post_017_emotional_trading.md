Let us talk about the trades you do not want to screenshot.

The revenge trades.
The "I will just make it back real quick" trades.
The "one more" trades after you already hit your daily loss limit.

Those rarely come from Sniper Pro, Quantum, or ATH. They usually come from:

- Frustration
- Ego
- Fear of missing out

Here is a simple rule you can borrow:

If you feel any spike of emotion, you **must**:

1. Step away from the screen for five minutes
2. Write down what you are feeling
3. Only take a trade if it matches a setup you already have written in your plan

No plan on paper means no trade.

Your account does not care how you feel. It only reflects what you do.

If the last few weeks have had too many emotional decisions, let this be the week you start checking in with yourself **before** you click.

Comment "CALM" if you are ready to trade like a pro, not like a slot machine player.
