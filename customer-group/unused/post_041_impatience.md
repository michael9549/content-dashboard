# The Emotion That's Sabotaging Your Trades (It's Not Fear)

Everyone talks about fear in trading.

Fear of losing. Fear of missing out. Fear of being wrong.

But there's another emotion that destroys more accounts than fear ever will:

Impatience.

---

**Impatience makes you:**

→ Enter before your setup is complete
→ Move your stop because "it's taking too long"
→ Close winners early because you want the money now
→ Overtrade because you "need" to make something happen today

Fear protects you.
Impatience destroys you.

---

**The market doesn't care about your timeline.**

It moves when it moves.
Your job is to be ready when it does.
Not to force it before it's ready.

The traders who win understand this:

Some days, the best trade is no trade.
Some weeks, the best move is to wait.
Some months, the growth happens in your learning, not your account.

---

**Patience isn't passive. It's strategic.**

It's knowing when to act and when to sit still.
It's trusting that your edge will show up if you wait for it.
