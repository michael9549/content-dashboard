# You're Probably Overcomplicating Your Trading

How many indicators are on your charts right now?

Be honest.

5? 8? 12?

And how often do they contradict each other?

One says buy. One says sell. Another says wait. So you freeze. Or worse, you guess.

---

**More indicators doesn't mean more edge.**

It usually means more confusion. More hesitation. More second-guessing.

The best traders I know use simple setups. Clean charts. Clear rules.

They don't need confirmation from 6 different tools. They know what they're looking for.

---

**Complexity is a trap.**

It feels like you're being thorough. Like you're covering all your bases.

But really, you're just hiding behind tools because you don't trust your own judgment yet.

---

**Here's the fix:**

Strip your chart down to the essentials.

What actually matters for YOUR strategy?

Keep that. Remove everything else.

Trade with it for 30 days. No exceptions. No adding "just one more indicator."

You'll be shocked how much clearer your decisions become.

---

**Simple scales. Complex fails.**

The market rewards clarity, not confusion.

Less is more. Always.
