# FOMO Is Costing You More Than You Think

You see a move happening.

It's already up 50 pips. Breaking out. Everyone's talking about it.

Your finger hovers over the buy button.

"I can't miss this," you think.

So you chase it.

And immediately it reverses. Stops you out. Then continues without you.

---

**FOMO is the silent account killer.**

It doesn't feel like a mistake in the moment. It feels like opportunity.

But here's what FOMO actually is:

It's trading based on emotion, not your plan.
It's entering late, with worse risk/reward.
It's abandoning your rules because you feel left out.

---

**The traders who last know this secret:**

There will always be another trade.

Always.

Missing one move doesn't matter. Taking a bad trade because you were afraid to miss out? That matters a lot.

---

**How to beat FOMO:**

1. Remind yourself: "My setup, my timing, or no trade."
2. Ask: "Would I take this trade if it wasn't already moving?"
3. Remember all the times chasing cost you money.
4. Trust that your edge will show up again tomorrow.

---

**The market isn't going anywhere.**

But your account might be if you keep trading from FOMO.

---

**Drop a 🎯 if you're committing to skipping trades that don't fit YOUR plan.**

Your patience will pay you more than your urgency ever will.
