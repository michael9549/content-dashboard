# You Can't See Your Own Blind Spots

Here's a hard truth:

The mistakes that are costing you money right now?
You probably don't even know you're making them.

We all have blind spots.
Patterns we repeat. Habits we don't notice. Excuses we've normalized.

And left alone, we'll keep making the same mistakes for years.

---

**This is why community matters.**

Not for signals.
Not for hot tips.

For the moments when someone says:
"Hey, I've noticed you always say you're going to do X, but then you do Y."

That one comment can change everything.

Because once you see the pattern, you can break it.
But you can't see it alone.

---

**The traders who level up fastest aren't lone wolves.**

They're surrounded by people who:
→ Call them out with love
→ Share what they're seeing
→ Hold them accountable
→ Celebrate the small wins

You don't need more information.
You need more perspective.
