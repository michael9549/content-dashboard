# The Best Traders I Know Keep It Stupid Simple

Here's what separates consistently profitable traders from the ones stuck in analysis paralysis:

They don't have the most complex strategy.
They have the simplest one they actually follow.

I've watched traders with "perfect" systems fail because they couldn't execute consistently.
And I've watched traders with basic setups win because they took the same trade, the same way, every single time.

Complexity feels smart.
Simplicity makes money.

---

**Your strategy doesn't need more indicators.**

It needs:
→ Clear entry rules you can explain in one sentence
→ A stop loss you set without hesitation
→ A target that makes sense for your account
→ The discipline to walk away when there's no setup

That's it.

The market rewards consistency, not cleverness.

---

**Ask yourself:**

Could you teach your strategy to a complete beginner in 5 minutes?

If not, it might be too complicated.

The traders who compound their accounts aren't doing anything fancy.
They're just doing the basics better than everyone else.
