# Consistency Beats Intensity (Every Single Time)

The trader who makes 2% per week beats the trader who makes 20% in one day and gives it all back.

Every. Single. Time.

---

We glorify the big wins.

The screenshots. The "I caught the whole move" stories. The massive percentage gains.

But nobody posts about the trader who quietly makes 8% per month, every month, for a year.

That trader? They just doubled their account. With less stress. Less drama. Less risk.

---

**Intensity is sexy. Consistency is profitable.**

Anyone can get lucky once. Very few can produce results repeatedly.

The market doesn't reward heroes. It rewards survivors.

---

**What consistency actually looks like:**

Taking your setups, even when you don't feel like it.

Walking away when your rules say to stop.

Not chasing trades just because you're bored.

Doing the boring work, day after day.

---

**Your goal shouldn't be to hit a home run.**

It should be to get on base consistently. The home runs will come as a byproduct of staying in the game long enough.

Small wins compound. Big losses destroy.

---

**Comment "CONSISTENT" if you're done chasing big wins and ready to play the long game.**

The tortoise wins this race. Be the tortoise.
