# Community is the New Moat

In the age of AI, technical skills are becoming commoditized. Anyone can generate content, research, or build apps.

The new competitive advantage? Community.

It's no longer about what you know. It's about who you know and who you can reach.

That's why even the biggest podcasts now have Telegram channels and WhatsApp communities. They're building direct relationships.

A community is a group of like-minded people who believe what you believe. They follow what you teach. They evangelize what you sell.

The hard part isn't attracting people to your community. It's keeping them there.

What's your community's "stickiness factor"?
