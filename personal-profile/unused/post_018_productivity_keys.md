Keys to Increasing Productivity

From Brian Tracy, here are the fundamentals:

1. Develop clear goals and write them down
2. Write a clear action plan
3. Set your priorities
4. Concentrate and eliminate distractions
5. Lengthen your work day but increase time off
6. Work harder at what you do
7. Pick up the pace
8. Work smarter (focus on value, not hours)
9. Align your work with your skills
10. Bunch your tasks (batch similar activities)
11. Cut out steps in the process

The key insight: It's not activity, it's accomplishments that matter.
