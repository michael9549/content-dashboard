# 5 Sales Strategies for 2026

You can't be in business and hate selling. Everything you bought, somebody sold to you.

Here are 5 strategies to consider:

1. Evangelize — Use influence. Get credible people to speak about what you do.

2. Knowledge — Sell based on what you know that nobody else knows. Unique insights win.

3. Community — Build a group of like-minded people who believe what you believe. Community is the new moat.

4. Exclusivity — Make it hard to join. Scarcity drives desire.

5. Freemium — Give value first, then convert. But be careful. Free customers rarely want to pay.

Which one fits your business?
