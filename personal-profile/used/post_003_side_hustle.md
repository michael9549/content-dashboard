I stumbled across something interesting the other day.

A guy making over $1,000/month selling books on Amazon that he didn't even write.

Not ebooks. Not fiction novels. Not self-help books.

Low-content books. Think activity books. Dot-to-dot books. Journals. Planners.

And here's the crazy part: it's stupid simple.

**Here's the exact process:**

1. **Find a winning book on Amazon.** Look for books with low rankings (meaning they're selling well) and high reviews. These are proven sellers.

2. **Sign up for Amazon KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing).** It's free. You earn 60% royalties on every book sold. Amazon handles printing, shipping, and customer service.

3. **Get the interior template.** Go to CreativeF abrica and download a template for the type of book you want to create. Dot-to-dot? Coloring book? Activity book? They have it.

4. **Modify it on Canva.** Clean up the template. Remove extra stuff. Make it your own.

5. **Use a simple tool to create the content.** For dot-to-dot books, there's literally a free website (dottomaker.com) where you upload an image, place dots around it, and download the finished page. Repeat 30-40 times and you have a complete book.

6. **Upload to KDP and start selling.** Set your price. Amazon lists it. People buy it. You get paid.

That's it.

No writing. No complicated design skills. No inventory. No upfront cost.

And the best part? Once it's uploaded, it's PASSIVE.

You do the work once. It sells forever.

One book might make you $50/month. Create 10 books? That's $500/month. Build a catalog of 20-30? You're looking at real, consistent income.

Now, here's what most people will do:

They'll read this and think, "Eh, sounds too good to be true" or "I don't have time" or "I'll do it later."

And they'll stay exactly where they are.

But a few of you will actually DO IT.

You'll spend a weekend creating your first book. You'll upload it. You'll see your first sale. And then you'll create another. And another.

And in 6 months, you'll have a portfolio of books generating passive income while you sleep.

Amazon has 300 million active customers. The traffic is already there. You just have to show up with something people want.

So stop overthinking it.

Pick a niche. Make a book. Upload it. Repeat.

That's the game.
