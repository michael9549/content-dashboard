I was watching a video recently and came across something I had to share.

The creator was talking about How I became an entrepreneur at 66 | Paul Tasner, and one point really stood out.

They explained that success isn't about having everything figured out from the start. It's about taking consistent action even when you're uncertain.

Most people wait until they feel ready. They wait for the perfect moment, the perfect plan, the perfect circumstances. But that moment never comes.

The people who actually build something meaningful are the ones who start before they're ready. They take the first step, then figure out the second step as they go.
