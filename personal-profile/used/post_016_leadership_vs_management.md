Leadership vs Management

They're not the same. You need both.

Leadership is about vision. It's asking "What should we do next?" It's inspiring people toward a shared goal.

Management is about execution. It's asking "How do we do this efficiently?" It's making sure things get done.

Leaders focus on people. They develop, motivate, and empower.

Managers focus on processes. They ensure systems run smoothly and tasks get completed.

Leaders take risks. They innovate and push boundaries.

Managers minimize risk. They maintain stability and order.

The best professionals combine both. They can inspire a vision AND execute on it.
