Before your next big:
→ Job interview
→ Sales call
→ Presentation
→ Difficult conversation

Do THIS for 2 minutes:

Stand like Wonder Woman.

Feet apart. Hands on hips. Chest open.

Or sit with your arms behind your head, legs spread.

Why?

Because Harvard researcher Amy Cuddy discovered something incredible:

Your BODY LANGUAGE changes your HORMONES.

Here's what happens in just 2 minutes of "power posing":

✅ Testosterone (dominance hormone) increases by 20%
✅ Cortisol (stress hormone) decreases by 25%

Translation?

You become more confident, assertive, and calm under pressure.

In job interview studies:

→ People who power posed were rated MORE HIREABLE
→ They performed better under stress
→ They brought their REAL selves to the interview

The best part?

In the bathroom. In an elevator. Behind closed doors.

No one needs to know.
