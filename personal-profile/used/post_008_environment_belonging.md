# Why Your Environment Determines Your Success (The Science of Belonging)

Jay Bailey runs the largest center for Black entrepreneurs in the world.

But he didn't model it after typical business incubators.

He modeled it after Motown and HBCUs.

Why?

Because both had ONE thing in common:

They created spaces where people saw BRILLIANCE in their own reflection.

Here's what most people don't understand about success:

It's not just about skills.

It's not just about knowledge.

It's about BELONGING.

When you walk into a room and see people who look like you succeeding...

→ Your mindset shifts
→ Your confidence grows
→ Your possibilities expand

Jay noticed something in his MBA classes:

Some students entered like "Alphas" - taking up space, raising hands high.

Others literally "collapsed" - making themselves small, hands barely lifted.

The difference?

Participation grades.

And ultimately, success.

So he built the Russell Center to be different:

✅ A space rooted in BELONGING
✅ Where wellness matters as much as P&L statements
✅ Where entrepreneurs don't feel alone
✅ Where your authentic self is welcome

The results?

→ 360 entrepreneurs supported full-time
→ 1,500 new jobs created
→ $450M+ in economic value generated

Your environment is either:
→ Expanding your possibilities
→ Shrinking your potential

Choose your circles carefully.

Surround yourself with people who make you BELIEVE.

Who's in YOUR circle?
