# 6 Mindsets That Break ALL the Business Rules (And Win)

Business school teaches you to "stick to your knitting."

Focus on what you're good at. Say NO to everything else.

But the most successful entrepreneurs?

They do the EXACT opposite.

Here are 6 "anti-conventional" mindsets that built billion-dollar companies:

1️⃣ "YES, WE CAN" MINDSET
When customers ask for something outside your expertise, say YES first, figure it out later.

Arnold Correa built Atmo Digital by saying "yes" to satellite technology he'd never used.

2️⃣ PROBLEM-FIRST, NOT PRODUCT-FIRST
Don't focus on your product. Focus on the PROBLEM.

Jonathan Thorn built a surgical tool company by solving a problem surgeons didn't even know they had.

3️⃣ THINK NARROW, NOT BROAD
Nike started with elite distance runners. Not everyone. Not all athletes.

ONE specific problem. ONE specific group.

Master that, then expand.

4️⃣ ASK FOR MONEY BEFORE YOU BUILD
Tesla sold 100 Roadsters for $100K each BEFORE building a single car.

$10M in the bank. Zero product.

That's how you fund a startup.

5️⃣ BEG, BORROW, BUT DON'T STEAL
Go Ape borrowed trees, parking lots, and locations from the UK Forestry Commission.

They built 30+ locations without owning land.

6️⃣ DON'T ASK PERMISSION
Uber didn't ask regulators if they could start.

They just started.

Sometimes you have to move first and apologize later.
